This system navigates the users through the following three kinds of contents;

1.    Definition of disease based on the River Flow Model of disease
- Navigating definitions of diseases based on causal chains with links to other systems/datasets.
    Examples) Mitral stenosis

2.    General causal chains in a human body
- Browsing possible causal chains of abnormal state (disorder) in human body.
    Examples) heart failure

3.      Definition of abnormal state (clinical disorder)
- Browsing is-a hierarchy of abnormal state (clinical disorder) otology with mappings to other resources.
    Examples) heart failure
